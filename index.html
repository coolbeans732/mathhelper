Standard Math Curriculum (Webb City Middle School)

🧮 Numbers & Operations

  • Place value and number sense
  • Operations with whole numbers, decimals, fractions
  • Ratios, rates, and percents
  • Prime factorization, GCF, LCM

📐 Geometry

  • Area, perimeter, surface area, volume
  • Properties of 2D and 3D shapes
  • Angle relationships
  • Coordinate plane geometry
  • Transformations: translations, rotations, reflections, dilations

📊 Data & Statistics

  • Mean, median, mode, range
  • Box plots, histograms, dot plots
  • Probability: simple and compound
  • Interpreting graphs and tables

🧠 Algebraic Thinking

  • Expressions and equations
  • Solving one- and two-step equations
  • Inequalities
  • Patterns and sequences
  • Linear relationships and graphing

📈 Functions (Grade 8)

  • Function notation
  • Comparing linear and nonlinear functions
  • Slope and rate of change
  • Systems of equations
