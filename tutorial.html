Math Tutorial Hub

Number Sense

Decimals: A decimal is just a way to show part of a whole. For example, 0.5 means half. You line up the decimal points when adding or subtracting.

Fractions: Fractions like 3/4 mean 3 parts out of 4. You can convert them to decimals by dividing: 3 ÷ 4 = 0.75.

Percents: Percent means "out of 100." So 25% of 80 is 0.25 × 80 = 20.

Algebra

Variables: A variable is just a letter that stands for a number. If x + 3 = 7, then x must be 4.

Expressions: An expression like 2x + 5 means “two times something, plus five.” You can plug in numbers to test it.

Equations: Solving equations means finding the number that makes both sides equal.

Geometry

Area: Area means how much space is inside a shape. For rectangles, it’s length × width.

Perimeter: Perimeter is the distance around a shape. Add up all the sides.

Angles: A right angle is 90°. A straight line is 180°. Angles measure turns.
